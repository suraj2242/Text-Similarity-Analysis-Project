
Indian politics is a kaleidoscope of diverse personalities, ideologies, and controversies. Some of the most prominent figures in the contemporary political landscape are Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Rahul Gandhi of the Indian National Congress, regional leaders like Mamata Banerjee and Arvind Kejriwal, and various ministers holding powerful portfolios. These politicians play a critical role in shaping the country's trajectory, making headlines both for their actions and their missteps.
